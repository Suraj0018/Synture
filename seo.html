Content Strategy Framework

Develop a systematic approach to content creation that aligns with search intent and business objectives.

1

Keyword Research & Intent Mapping

Identify high-value keywords and categorize them by search intent (informational, navigational, commercial, transactional).

Tools: SEMrush, Ahrefs, Google Keyword Planner

2

Content Gap Analysis

Analyze competitor content to identify opportunities where you can provide more comprehensive coverage.

Metrics: Word count, backlinks, content freshness, multimedia usage

3

Topic Cluster Development

Create pillar pages and supporting content that establishes authority on key topics.

Strategy: 1 pillar page (2,000+ words) with 5-10 cluster pages (800-1,500 words)

4

Content Optimization

Enhance existing content with updated information, improved structure, and multimedia elements.

Focus: Readability, semantic SEO, internal linking, CTAs
