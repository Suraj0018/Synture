Sector-Specific Trends

The tech hiring landscape varies significantly across different sectors. While traditional software companies are slowing hiring, emerging fields like clean tech and health tech are experiencing rapid growth.

Fintech companies continue to lead in compensation packages, offering 12-15% above market average for specialized roles. Meanwhile, the gaming industry shows the highest turnover rate at 22% annually.

Key Takeaways

Based on our analysis of over 500 companies and 10,000 job postings, here are the most significant findings:

1

Skills Over Degrees

67% of employers now prioritize demonstrable skills over formal education, a 22% increase from 2020. Certification programs have become 3x more valuable in the hiring process.

2

Contract Work Rising

Contract tech roles grew by 28% as companies seek flexibility. The average contract duration decreased from 12 to 8 months, indicating faster project cycles.

3

Global Talent Competition

Borderless hiring increased by 40%, creating intense competition for top talent. Companies in emerging markets offer competitive packages to retain local experts.
