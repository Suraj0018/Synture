Algorithm Changes

Understanding how platform algorithms prioritize content in 2025

  • Meaningful Interactions: Posts that spark conversations between friends (comments and replies) get significantly more reach.
  • Video Watch Time: Videos that keep viewers watching past 1 minute receive a major boost in distribution.
  • Link Posts Penalized: External links in posts see reduced reach compared to native content.
  • Group Content Prioritized: Posts in active groups appear higher in members' feeds.
  • Reels Dominance: The algorithm heavily favors Reels, especially those using trending audio and effects.
  • Original Content Boost: Unique content (not repurposed from other platforms) gets more distribution.
  • Engagement Speed: Posts that get quick likes/comments in the first 30 minutes perform better.
  • Carousel Preference: Multi-image posts now outperform single images in most cases.
  • Comment Engagement: Posts with thoughtful comments (not just reactions) get more visibility.
  • Long-form Content: Articles and detailed posts perform better than short updates.
  • Video Growth: Native videos see 5x more reach than external links to videos.
  • Industry Relevance: Content is increasingly shown to users in related industries.
  • Watch Completion: Videos with high completion rates (especially rewatching) rank higher.
  • New Creator Boost: The algorithm gives new accounts more visibility in their first 30 days.
  • Trend Participation: Using trending sounds/challenges increases discoverability.
  • Share Metric: Videos that get shared (especially in DMs) get prioritized.
